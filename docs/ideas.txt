A web app to preview the deck_info.json from 2anki.net. Also it should enable the uploader to make corrections by picking out new selectors for the front / back and then trigger the conversion / inspect again.

Problem

The problem with 2anki.net is that some people get surprised by the converted output. Also they have
expectations on what is supported. The inspecter will help them learn how to use 2anki while improving
it for their usecases.
